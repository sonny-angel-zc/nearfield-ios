# Nearfield Sonic — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 28, 2026

## Overview

Nearfield Sonic is a proximity-based sound app that uses Apple's Nearby Interaction framework and Multipeer Connectivity to create shared audio experiences between nearby devices.

## Data We Collect

**None.** Nearfield Sonic does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

## What Stays on Your Device

- **Proximity data** (distance and direction to nearby peers) is calculated locally using Apple's Nearby Interaction framework and is never sent to any server.
- **Device name** is shared only with nearby peers via Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework for the duration of a session.
- **Microphone audio** (in Grainfield mode) is processed locally and streamed only to connected peers over the local network. No audio is recorded or stored.

## Third-Party Services

Nearfield Sonic does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Network Communication

All peer-to-peer communication occurs over the local network using Apple's Multipeer Connectivity framework with required encryption. No data is sent to external servers.

## Children's Privacy

Nearfield Sonic does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.
